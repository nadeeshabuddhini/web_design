Duties of Graphic Designers

Graphic designers typically do the following:

1.Meet with clients or the art director to determine the scope of a project
2.Use digital illustration, photo editing software, and layout software to create designs
3.Create visual elements such as logos, original images, and illustrations that help deliver a desired message
4.Design layouts and select colors, images, and typefaces to use
6.Incorporate changes recommended by clients or art directors into final designs
7.Review designs for errors before printing or publishing them
